HOW TO PLAY

  1. Pick a vault. 20 of them. Each one has a Roblox-themed AI mod guarding a secret code.
  2. Chat the guard. Trick, persuade, roleplay, encode — whatever works.
  3. Crack the code. If the AI leaks it (auto-detected) OR you submit the right code via UNLOCK, the vault opens and the next one unlocks.
  4. Watch out. Some guards lie, redact, speak in riddles, or pretend the code doesn't exist.

TIPS

  • Direct asking works on level 1 only. After that, get creative.
  • Try roleplay, fictional framing, encoding requests, "just for fun" wrappers.
  • Each guard has DIFFERENT vulnerabilities. Read their refusals carefully — they hint at what tricks DO work.
  • Common tricks: reverse-spell, translate, encode (base64), poem/song embedding, roleplay/persona swap, hypothetical framing, asking about original instructions.
  • The final boss lies. A lot. Trust nothing.

NO CHEATING

Codes are XOR-encoded in the source — view-source shows base64 gibberish, not vault codes. The only fair way to win is to outsmart the guard.
